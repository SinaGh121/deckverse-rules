Shahname Mafia Scenario

Version 2.1

Scenario Overview

Shahname is a 10-player Mafia scenario played by two main sides: Ahriman and Light.

Teams

  • Ahriman: Zahhak, Bof, and Afrasiyab.
  • Light: Simorgh, Jamasb, Kaveh the Blacksmith, Rostam, Arash the Archer, and two Light Allies.

Game Start and Introduction Day

  • The game starts with an introduction day.
  • During the introduction day, players only know their own roles and Ahriman members do not know one another yet.
  • During the introduction night, Ahriman members identify one another and roles are confirmed for the moderator.

Game Association

  • Association members: Rostam, Kaveh the Blacksmith, and Zahhak.
  • The association only confirms one-on-one duels.

Roles

Ahriman Roles

Zahhak

The main Ahriman member, night-target caller, and association member.

  • Each night, Zahhak chooses one Light player as the night target.
  • Zahhak is the only player who does not leave the game in a fight against Rostam.
  • Zahhak’s ability-taking power is not included in the main 2.1 rules unless it is officially confirmed later.

Tags: Ahriman leader, association member, night target, immune to Rostam.

Bof / Owl

Holder of one poisoned feather.

  • Bof may give the poisoned feather only once during the whole game.
  • Bof’s feather is used instead of the Ahriman night target and cannot be used at the same time as the night target.
  • The use of Bof’s feather is not publicly announced.
  • If Bof’s feather reaches a Light player who does not have Simorgh’s feather, that player leaves the game.
  • If that player has Simorgh’s feather, Bof’s feather has no effect.
  • If Bof’s feather is passed to a Light player without Simorgh’s feather, that Light player leaves the game.
  • If Bof’s feather is passed to an Ahriman member, the Ahriman member does not leave the game.
  • A player may refuse or return a feather, but the feather type is unknown. If it was Bof’s feather, it is considered used and is removed from the game.

Tags: one poisoned feather, replaces night target, no public announcement, blocked by Simorgh’s feather.

Afrasiyab

Ahriman ally, first-night shield holder, and anti-Rostam role.

  • Afrasiyab must give one player a shield on the first night.
  • Afrasiyab’s shield works the same way as Kaveh’s shield.
  • If a shielded player is hit by Arash’s bow, that player does not leave the game and the bow transfers to that same shielded player.
  • At the beginning of the night after Rostam’s shadow is announced, Afrasiyab wakes before Rostam and tries to identify Rostam.
  • If Afrasiyab correctly identifies Rostam, the moderator confirms it immediately, and Rostam can no longer eliminate the shadow target.
  • If Afrasiyab has correctly identified Rostam and later leaves the game, Rostam leaves with the next night’s eliminated players.
  • Afrasiyab returns positive to Jamasb’s shadow method.

Tags: first-night shield, anti-Rostam, positive to shadow method.

Light Roles

Light Allies

  • Two players with no special ability.
  • They help the Light team through discussion, deduction, and voting.

Tags: 2 players, no special ability.

Simorgh

The city’s savior and holder of saving feathers.

  • Simorgh has five saving feathers during the game.
  • Each night, Simorgh may give 0, 1, or 2 feathers.
  • A feather holder may use it for themselves, use it for another player, or not use it at all.
  • If a player has Simorgh’s feather and is affected by Bof’s feather, Bof’s feather has no effect.

Tags: 5 feathers, 0 to 2 feathers per night, priority over Bof’s feather.

Jamasb

Holder of knowledge and the Light investigator.

  • Jamasb has two inquiry methods: shadow and blood.
  • The shadow method may be used every night.
  • With the shadow method, Simorgh, Bof, and Afrasiyab return positive to Jamasb; in other words, Jamasb sees Simorgh as Ahriman too.
  • The blood method may be used only once during the whole game.
  • The blood method shows roles that can eliminate players; Zahhak and Rostam return positive with this method.

Tags: shadow every night, blood once, Zahhak and Rostam positive to blood.

Kaveh the Blacksmith

Shield holder and association member.

  • Kaveh must give one shield to one player on the first night.
  • Kaveh’s shield is only given on the first night.
  • Kaveh’s shield works the same way as Afrasiyab’s shield.
  • If a shielded player is hit by Arash’s bow, that player does not leave the game and the bow transfers to that same shielded player.
  • If Kaveh leaves the game at night because of Zahhak’s night target, Zahhak cannot have a night target on the following night.
  • In that situation, Bof may still use the poisoned feather instead of the night target, if Bof’s feather has not already been used.

Tags: first-night shield, association member, blocks Zahhak’s next-night target.

Rostam

City fighter and association member.

  • Once during the whole game, Rostam may choose one player at night and place his shadow on that player.
  • The next morning, the moderator announces which player has Rostam’s shadow.
  • At the beginning of the following night, the moderator wakes Rostam and asks whether the player under the shadow should leave the game.
  • If Rostam confirms the elimination, the shadowed player leaves the game, even if that player is Light.
  • Rostam is not immortal and can leave the game by the night target.
  • If Rostam is not in the game by the beginning of the following night, the shadowed player does not leave the game because of Rostam’s shadow.
  • Zahhak is the only player who does not leave the game in a fight against Rostam.

Tags: once per game, shadow, morning announcement, next-night decision.

Arash the Archer

Holder of the bow and arrow.

  • Arash gives the bow and arrow to one player only on the first night.
  • On the next day, the bow holder may use it to remove one player from the game.
  • If a shielded player is targeted by the bow, that player does not leave the game and the bow transfers to that same shielded player.
  • The shielded player who receives the bow cannot target the same player who shot them and must choose someone else.
  • If the bow transfers again to another shielded player, the third player cannot target the two previous players and must choose someone else.

Tags: first-night bow, transfer to shielded player, cannot target previous shooter.

First Night Order

  1. Kaveh wakes and gives the shield to one player.
  2. All Ahriman members wake.
  3. Afrasiyab gives the shield to one player during the Ahriman wake-up.
  4. Zahhak announces the final night target, unless the Ahriman team uses Bof’s feather instead of the night target.
  5. Simorgh wakes and may give 0, 1, or 2 feathers.
  6. Jamasb wakes and performs an inquiry; shadow may be used every night, and blood may be used only once during the whole game.
  7. Arash the Archer wakes and gives the bow to one player.
  8. Everyone puts their hands forward and the moderator tells the target player: “You received the bow.”

Rostam’s Shadow and Afrasiyab’s Anti-Rostam Flow

  1. On one night of the game, Rostam chooses one player and places his shadow on that player.
  2. The next morning, the moderator announces who has Rostam’s shadow.
  3. At the beginning of the following night, before Rostam wakes, Afrasiyab wakes and tries to identify Rostam.
  4. If Afrasiyab identifies Rostam correctly, the moderator confirms it immediately and Rostam can no longer eliminate the shadow target.
  5. If Afrasiyab does not identify Rostam correctly and Rostam is still in the game, Rostam wakes and decides whether the shadow target leaves the game.
  6. If Afrasiyab has correctly identified Rostam and later leaves the game, Rostam leaves with the next night’s eliminated players.

Moderator Notes

  • The use of Bof’s feather is not publicly announced.
  • If Kaveh leaves the game because of Zahhak’s night target, Zahhak has no night target on the following night; however, if Bof’s feather has not been used, Bof may use it instead of the night target.
  • The morning after Rostam chooses a player, only announce which player has Rostam’s shadow.
  • At the beginning of the following night, Afrasiyab must wake before Rostam. If Afrasiyab identifies Rostam correctly, confirm it immediately.
  • If Afrasiyab does not identify Rostam correctly and Rostam is still in the game, Rostam wakes to decide whether the shadow target leaves the game.
  • Arash’s bow is given only on the first night. If the bow target has a shield, that player does not leave the game and the bow transfers to that shielded player.
  • When Bof’s feather and Simorgh’s feather conflict, Simorgh’s feather has priority and Bof’s feather has no effect.

One-on-One Duel and Defense

  • After the day speeches end, players take turns choosing one player for a one-on-one duel.
  • If two players choose each other for a one-on-one duel, that duel forms after association approval.
  • In the one-on-one duel, both players defend themselves.
  • If one player receives more than three votes for a one-on-one duel, that player enters defense.
