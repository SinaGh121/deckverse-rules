Shahname Mafia Scenario

Version 2.0

Scenario Overview

Shahname is a 10-player Mafia scenario played by two main sides: Ahriman and Light.

The scenario is built around roles inspired by Shahname and the conflict between Zahhak, Bof, and Afrasiyab against the Light roles.

Teams

  • Ahriman: Zahhak, Bof, and Afrasiyab.
  • Light: Simorgh, Jamasb, Kaveh the Blacksmith, Rostam, Arash the Archer, and two Light Allies.

Game Start and Blind Day

  • Based on the earlier confirmation, the game starts with a blind day.
  • During the blind day, players only know their own roles and Ahriman members do not know one another yet.
  • During the introduction night, Ahriman members identify one another and roles are confirmed for the moderator.

Game Association

  • Association members: Rostam, Kaveh the Blacksmith, and Zahhak.
  • The association only confirms one-on-one duels.

Roles

Ahriman Roles

Zahhak

Main Ahriman member, night-kill caller, and association member.

  • Each night, one Light player is killed by Zahhak’s choice.
  • Zahhak is the only player who does not leave the game in a fight against Rostam.

Tags: Ahriman leader, association member, night kill, immune to Rostam’s fight.

Bof

Omen of misfortune and holder of the poisoned feather.

  • During one night of the game, Bof gives the poisoned feather to one player.
  • If the poisoned feather is used on a Light member, that player leaves the game.
  • Simorgh’s feather always has priority over Bof’s feather.

Tags: one poisoned feather, feather elimination, Simorgh priority.

Afrasiyab

Ahriman ally and holder of the first-night shield.

  • On the first night, Afrasiyab gives one player a shield.
  • Afrasiyab’s shield works the same way as Kaveh’s shield.
  • If a player with Afrasiyab’s shield is hit by Arash’s bow, that player does not leave the game and the ability to use the bow transfers to that shielded player.
  • If Afrasiyab correctly chooses Rostam, Rostam’s fight ability is removed.
  • If Afrasiyab has correctly chosen Rostam, Rostam also leaves the game when Afrasiyab leaves.
  • Afrasiyab returns positive to Jamasb’s shadow method.

Tags: first-night shield, anti-Rostam, positive to shadow method.

Light Roles

Light Allies

Two players with no special ability.

  • Light Allies have no special day or night power.
  • They help the Light team through discussion and deduction.

Tags: 2 players, no special ability.

Simorgh

The first Light role and the city’s savior.

  • Simorgh has five saving feathers during the game.
  • Simorgh may give these feathers on different nights.
  • Players who receive a feather may use it for themselves or another player, or choose not to use it.
  • Simorgh’s feather always has priority over Bof’s feather.

Tags: 5 feathers, given on different nights, priority over Bof’s feather.

Jamasb

Holder of knowledge and the Light investigator.

  • Each night, Jamasb may ask the moderator about one player using either the shadow method or the blood method.
  • With the shadow method, Simorgh, Bof, and Afrasiyab return positive to Jamasb.
  • With the blood method, Zahhak and Rostam return positive to Jamasb.
  • No separate limit is currently recorded for the blood method because the current transcript does not state one explicitly.

Tags: shadow method, blood method, nightly inquiry.

Kaveh the Blacksmith

Uprising figure, city sacrifice, shield holder, and association member.

  • Kaveh has one shield.
  • At night, Kaveh gives the shield to one player.
  • Kaveh’s shield works the same way as Afrasiyab’s shield.
  • If a player with Kaveh’s shield is hit by Arash’s bow, that player does not leave the game and the ability to use the bow transfers to that shielded player.
  • If Kaveh is killed by the Ahriman team at night, then on the following night, if Zahhak is still present in the Ahriman team, the Ahriman team loses its ability to kill.

Tags: one shield, association member, blocks next-night kill if killed by Ahriman.

Rostam

City fighter and association member.

  • On one night of the game, Rostam chooses one player to fight him.
  • On the following night, if Rostam recognizes correctly, the player chosen to fight Rostam leaves the game.
  • Zahhak is the only player who does not leave the game in a fight against Rostam.
  • The exact meaning of “if Rostam recognizes correctly” still needs confirmation.

Tags: city fighter, one night choice, association member.

Arash the Archer

Holder of the bow.

  • On the first night, Arash gives one player the bow.
  • On the next day, the bow holder may use it to remove one player from the game.
  • If a player with a shield is hit by the bow, that player does not leave the game.
  • In that case, the ability to use the bow transfers to the shielded player.

Tags: first-night bow, bow elimination, transfer to shielded player.

First Night Order

  1. Kaveh wakes and gives the shield to one player.
  2. All Ahriman members wake.
  3. Afrasiyab gives the shield to one player during the Ahriman wake-up.
  4. Zahhak announces the final night-kill target.
  5. Simorgh wakes and, if using a feather that night, gives the feather.
  6. Jamasb wakes and performs an inquiry using either the shadow method or the blood method.
  7. Arash the Archer wakes and gives the bow to one player.
  8. Everyone puts their hands forward and the moderator tells the target player: “You received the bow.”

In the current transcript, Rostam is described as choosing a player “on one night of the game”; therefore, Rostam’s wake-up was removed from the confirmed first-night order.

One-on-One Duel and Defense

  • After the day speeches end, players take turns choosing one player for a one-on-one duel.
  • If two players choose each other for a one-on-one duel, that duel forms after association approval.
  • In the one-on-one duel, both players defend themselves.
  • If one player receives more than three votes for a one-on-one duel, that player enters defense.
